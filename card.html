Quokka

They are known for being cute and smiley all the time

  • Scientific Name: Setonix brachyurus
  • Conservation Status: Vulnerable
  • Height: 9.8 to 11.8 in
  • Width: 2.5 to 5 kg

Quokkas are native to Australia and are found in many areas of the North and Western parts of the continent. Many are very approachable, but they are known to bite people who sometimes get too excited to take the infamous "Quokka Selfie" with the adorable creature.
